# FinPath
## Problem Statement:
Financial literacy remains a significant challenge, especially in underprivileged and marginalized communities. With limited access to financial education, these communities often face difficulties managing personal finances, budgeting, and saving for the future. The lack of accessible and understandable financial guidance leads to a cycle of poor financial decisions, contributing to economic instability and limited opportunities for upward mobility.

## Solution:
FinPath aims to solve this problem by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly financial literacy platform powered by advanced AI models like ChatGPT, Mistral, and Llama. This platform is designed to cater to individuals from all backgrounds, offering tailored financial advice, budgeting tools, and educational resources in a simple and accessible manner. By leveraging AI, FinPath provides real-time, personalized financial insights, helping users track their income, expenses, and savings goals.

## Key features include:

- Personalized Financial Dashboard: Users can access a snapshot of their financial health, including income, expenses, and savings, with easy-to-understand visualizations.
- AI-Powered Financial Advice: The platform uses AI to offer tailored financial advice based on user data, helping them make informed decisions about their finances.
- Educational Modules: A series of interactive lessons and learning modules to enhance financial literacy.
- Inclusive Design: The platform is built with accessibility in mind, ensuring it is usable for people of all abilities, including those with limited tech experience.
- FinPath aims to empower users to take control of their financial futures, reduce the knowledge gap, and promote financial well-being in underserved communities.
